Scott's Favourite Movies

My Top 5 Movies of All Time

Arn: The Knight Templar

Great historical fiction movie with beautiful cinematography and a compelling story.

Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

Masterpiece of fantasy filmmaking with incredible world-building and character development.

Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

Continuation of the epic tale with even more breathtaking visuals and deeper character development.

Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The triumphant conclusion to the trilogy with outstanding performances and a satisfying resolution.

The Greatest Showman

Excellent musical with great performances and a powerful message about following your dreams.
